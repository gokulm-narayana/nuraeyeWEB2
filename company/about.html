About Us

NuraEye proposed an AI Video surveillance and security systems brand powered by OnChip Technologies India Pvt. Ltd, Nuraeye set to launch in Hyderabad, serving nearby cities/regions in Telangana & then later in Pan India.

The business aims to provide high-quality CCTV products and professional installation services to residential and commercial clients. By leveraging advanced technology and superior customer service, NuraEye seeks to address the growing demand for security solutions in the region. The business will focus on offering a comprehensive range CCTV systems, including high-definition cameras, recording equipment, and installation services, ensuring robust security solutions for a wide range of clients.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, reliable, and smart surveillance systems to protect homes, businesses, and public infrastructure.

Our Vision

To make world-class smarter security solutions accessible to every home and business, ensuring safety through user-friendly, affordable, and dependable CCTV systems.

Why Nuraeye?

Incidents don’t wait for the perfect moment—they happen in seconds. NuraEye CCTV Surveillance provides real-time visibility across your security infrastructure, enabling teams to detect threats early and respond before situations escalate.
