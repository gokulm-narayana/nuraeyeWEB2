Nuraeye Guardpro 2 MP AI Smart Security Camera

Advanced AI Surveillance for Smarter Security

The Nuraeye’s Guardpro 4MP Smart Security Camera delivers powerful AI-driven monitoring with crystal-clear 2K resolution, intelligent detection, and full-color night vision. Designed for homes, offices, retail stores, and industrial facilities, it provides reliable, real-time security with instant alerts and remote access.

With advanced AI detection, two-way audio, cloud storage, and ONVIF compatibility, NuraEye ensures complete visibility and protection day and night.
